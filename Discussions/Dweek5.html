WEEK FIVE DISCUSSION

Discussion Topic:
After reading the different code of Ethics and Professional Conduct
Why is ethics an important part of the profession and you as a student?

My Reply:
Ethics plays an important role in software development, and this importance extends to students studying software development. Ethical considerations ensure that developers create products that improve the well-being of society rather than harm it. Ethical software development creates trust between the developers and its users. As a student aspiring to become a software developer, understanding and practicing ethical principles is a fundamental part of my professional development. It reflects my commitment to professionalism and responsible conduct in my career.
