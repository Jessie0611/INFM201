Define "SDLC" - Software Development Life Cycle.
Explain the steps of SDLC.
Why is it essential to know about HCI and SDLC when coding applications?

SDLCprovides a structured approach to software development. It outlines a series of phases that help developers through the entire project. This structure ensure that the project stays organized.

By integrating HCI aspects, developers can ensure that the application not only meets technical requirements but also supports the business goals by providing value to users. HCI leads to better UX design with interfaces that are visually appealing, responsive, and easy to navigate.
